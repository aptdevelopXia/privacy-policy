Introduction

Thank you for using APP NAME ("the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration or login.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information with us or any third parties.

The App operates primarily by connecting to compatible Bluetooth devices and processing information locally on your device.

Bluetooth Permission

The App requests Bluetooth permission to:

  • Discover compatible Bluetooth devices
  • Pair and communicate with connected devices
  • Read device information and activity data from connected devices

Bluetooth access is used solely to provide the App's core functionality and is not used for advertising, analytics, or user tracking.

Location Permission

The App requests location permission to provide turn-by-turn navigation functionality.

Location information is used only while navigation features are active to:

  • Determine your current position
  • Provide route guidance
  • Display navigation instructions
  • Update route progress during navigation

Location data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Data Storage

Ride records, Bluetooth device information, navigation data, and application preferences are stored locally on your device.

The App does not upload or synchronize this information to external servers.

Data Sharing

The App does not share, sell, rent, or disclose user information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because the App does not collect or store user information on external servers, all information remains under the user's control on their device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately upon posting.
